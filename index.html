Feeling stuck?

Choose one of your characters and answer these questions about them.
Don't think too hard about it - write the first thing that comes to your head.

  • What sort of shoes does your character wear?
  • What's in their pocket or bag?
  • Where do they live?
  • What is their job or occupation?
  • What is their name?
  • What are they secretly most afraid of? Why?
  • What does your character want...
  • ...and what is stopping them from getting it?
  • Get started describing how your character attempts to reach their goal.
  • Do they get what they want? It's up to you!
